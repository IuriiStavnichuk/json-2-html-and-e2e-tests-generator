    How Payza Works

    Personal accounts for Buyers

    Shop, send and get money easily!

    Load your E-Wallet

    Add funds from your bank account, credit card, by certified check or money order.

    Send or request money

    You can send money to or request money from anyone with just their email address.

    Let us do the rest

    Send your money, not your personal details. Payza keeps your information safe.

    Business accounts for Sellers

    Get paid easily!

    Accept payments

    Shoppers use our platform to pay you instantly and securely. Funds go right into your E-Wallet.

    Claim your money

    Withdraw money to your bank account, credit card or Payza prepaid card, or simply request a check.
